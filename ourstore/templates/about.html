About Sishwati Marie

Sishwati Marie is a brand that focuses on creating products such as leather, canvas, assorted handbags, clothing, and accessories. We aim to produce timeless quality products. Our pieces are handmade and sketched with unique designs by Founder, Sepo Sishwati. When you own a piece of Sishwati Marie, you not only own a functional piece of art but you also hold a piece of her story.

The meaning behind the name Sishwati Marie

Introducing Sishwati, a brand inspired by the strong women in my life. Named after my grandmother, a talented seamstress and pattern maker. Her name ‘Sishwati’, originating from the Lozi-speaking people of Barotseland in Zambia, means sweet like sugar cane. My late mother whose name was Mareba or sometimes called Maria, was a passionate journalist who loved playing with style and had a real love for clothes.

After losing my father, our lives changed drastically. My mother, a young widow, couldn’t afford much. We wore second-hand clothes and switched schools. Despite the challenges, my love for art and fashion was born.

Naming the brand after these powerful women only made sense and I had the privilege of being a part of their bloodline.
